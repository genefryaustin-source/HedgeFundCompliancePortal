# Data Classification & Handling Policy (SOC 2 Confidentiality & Privacy)

**Classification Levels**:
- **Public**: No restrictions.
- **Internal**: Company-only.
- **Confidential**: Client data, financials, business plans.
- **Highly Confidential**: PII, investor KYC/AML data, encryption keys.

**Handling Rules**:
- Storage: Encrypted at rest for Confidential and higher.
- Transmission: TLS 1.2+ or higher.
- Disposal: Secure shredding or cryptographic wipe.
- Labeling: Required on all documents and files.

**Annual Review**: Classification policy reviewed and updated yearly.
