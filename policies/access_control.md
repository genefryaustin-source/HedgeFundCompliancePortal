# Access Control Policy (SOC 2 Security Criterion)

**Purpose**: Ensure only authorized individuals have appropriate access to systems, data, and physical facilities.

**Scope**: All employees, contractors, vendors, and systems handling company or client data.

**Key Requirements**:
- Principle of Least Privilege: Users receive only the minimum access needed for their role.
- Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) implemented across all systems.
- Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) required for all accounts accessing sensitive data or production systems.
- Quarterly access reviews and recertification by managers.
- Immediate access revocation upon termination or role change.
- Segregation of Duties (SoD) for financial, compliance, and IT functions.
- Privileged access (admin/root) requires additional approval and logging.

**Enforcement**:
- All access requests must be documented and approved.
- Automated provisioning/de-provisioning integrated with HR system where possible.
- Annual policy review and employee attestation required.

**Responsibility**: Chief Compliance Officer (CCO) and IT Security Lead.
