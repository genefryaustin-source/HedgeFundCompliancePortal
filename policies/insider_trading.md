# Insider Trading & MNPI Policy (Section 204A of the Advisers Act)
**Policy**: Strict prohibition on trading or tipping while in possession of material non-public information (MNPI).

**Procedures**:
1. Immediate notification to CCO if MNPI received.
2. Restricted list maintenance.
3. Pre-clearance for all personal and firm trades.
4. Blackout periods around earnings or material events.
5. Annual training and attestation.
6. Surveillance of employee trading.

**Consequences**: Termination and potential regulatory reporting.

(Adapted from Seward & Kissel and SEC model policies.)
