# Code of Ethics – [Your Hedge Fund Group Name] (Rule 204A-1)
**Standards of Business Conduct**: All Access Persons must place client interests first, comply with federal securities laws, and avoid conflicts.

**Access Persons**: Portfolio managers, traders, CCO, anyone with access to MNPI or client trading information.

**Personal Securities Transactions**:
- Pre-clearance required for reportable securities.
- Quarterly transaction reports + initial/annual holdings reports.
- Blackout periods and restricted lists enforced.
- No frontrunning, dumping, or misuse of information.

**Gifts & Entertainment**: Pre-approval; de minimis limits.
**Political Contributions (Pay-to-Play)**: Monitored.
**Reporting Violations**: Promptly to CCO.

**Acknowledgement**: All supervised persons must attest annually via DocuSign.
