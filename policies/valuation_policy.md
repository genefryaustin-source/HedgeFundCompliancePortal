# Valuation Policy  
Adopted pursuant to Rule 206(4)-7 and fiduciary duty.

**Principles**: Fair value in accordance with GAAP/ASC 820; consistent, transparent, and independent where possible.

**Valuation Committee**: CCO + portfolio managers + independent member.
**Procedures**:
- Daily NAV for liquid holdings (market quotes).
- Monthly/quarterly for illiquid/alternatives: Independent pricing vendors, third-party valuation firms, or internal models with back-testing.
- Documentation: Valuation memos, supporting data, and challenges logged in portal.
- Disclosure: Material valuation policies in Form ADV Part 2 and investor reports.

**Oversight**: Annual review; independent auditor confirmation.
