# Custody & Safeguarding of Assets Policy (Rule 206(4)-2)
**Qualified Custodians**: Prime brokers and banks meeting Rule 206(4)-2 requirements.
**Procedures**:
- Assets held in client name or fund name; no commingling.
- Surprise examinations by independent public accountant if self-custody elements exist.
- Reconciliations performed monthly; discrepancies escalated immediately.
- Wire authorization: Dual approval + pre-verified instructions.

**Investor Reporting**: Quarterly statements delivered directly by custodian or administrator.
