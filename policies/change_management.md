# Change Management Policy (SOC 2 Security & Processing Integrity)

**Purpose**: Ensure all changes to systems, code, infrastructure, or configurations are authorized, tested, and documented to minimize risk.

**Scope**: All production environments, applications, infrastructure, and configurations.

**Process**:
1. Formal change request with business justification and risk assessment.
2. Testing in non-production environment.
3. Peer/code review and Change Advisory Board (CAB) approval for high-risk changes.
4. Deployment with documented rollback plan.
5. Post-implementation monitoring and review for 30 days.

**Emergency Changes**: Allowed with post-facto approval and full documentation within 24 hours.

**Recordkeeping**: All changes logged with approver, tester, and outcome for audit.

**Responsibility**: IT Lead and CCO.
