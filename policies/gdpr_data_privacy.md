# GDPR Data Privacy Policy  
[Your Hedge Fund Group Name] – Registered Investment Adviser ($1B AUM)  
Adopted to align with EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable (e.g., EU investors or data processors) and Reg S-P Safeguards Rule.

**Data Controller**: [Your Hedge Fund Group Name]  
**Data Protection Officer (DPO)**: Chief Compliance Officer (or designated delegate).

## 1. Principles (Art. 5 GDPR)
- Lawfulness, fairness, transparency  
- Purpose limitation  
- Data minimization  
- Accuracy  
- Storage limitation  
- Integrity & confidentiality  
- Accountability

## 2. Lawful Basis for Processing Investor & Employee Data
- Contract (subscription agreements, employment)  
- Legitimate interests (AML screening, KYC, performance reporting)  
- Legal obligation (AML/BSA, tax reporting)  
- Consent (where required — withdrawable)

## 3. Rights of Data Subjects (Art. 12–23)
- Right of access, rectification, erasure (“right to be forgotten”), restriction, portability, objection.  
- Requests handled within 30 days; logged in portal.

## 4. International Data Transfers
- EU → US: Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or Binding Corporate Rules.  
- Adequacy decisions monitored; Privacy Shield successor (if any) documented.

## 5. Data Breach Notification
- Internal: Report to DPO/CCO within 24 hours.  
- Supervisory Authority: Notify within 72 hours if risk to rights/freedoms.  
- Data Subjects: Direct notification if high risk.

## 6. Third-Party Processors & Vendor Due Diligence
- DPAs (Data Processing Agreements) required with all vendors (administrators, cloud providers).  
- Annual review of processor security (SOC 2, ISO 27001).

## 7. Record of Processing Activities (Art. 30)
- Maintained in Google Drive: categories of data, purposes, recipients, retention, safeguards.

## 8. Training & Awareness
- Annual GDPR module in Training Center (quiz + DocuSign attestation).

**Recordkeeping**: All policies, assessments, and data-subject requests retained 6 years (GDPR) or 5 years (SEC).  
**Annual Review**: CCO documents compliance effectiveness.
