# Marketing & Advertising Policy (Rule 206(4)-1)
**General Requirements**: All advertisements must be fair and balanced; no false or misleading claims.

**Testimonials/Endorsements**: Clear disclosures; no compensation if misleading.
**Hypothetical Performance**: Policies for relevance and presentation.
**Performance Advertising**: Net-of-fees; 1-, 5-, 10-year periods where applicable.
**Substantiation**: Records retained 5 years.

**Approval Process**: All marketing materials pre-approved by CCO; logged in Google Drive.

(Procedures include social media guidelines and placement agent oversight.)
