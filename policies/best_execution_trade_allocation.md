# Best Execution & Trade Allocation Policy
**Best Execution**: Regular broker reviews (execution quality, price, speed, likelihood of execution).
**Trade Allocation**:
- Fair and equitable across funds/accounts (pro-rata or rotational).
- No preferential treatment; side-by-side management conflicts disclosed.
- Pre-trade allocation policy documented.

**Monitoring**: CCO reviews trade blotter monthly; personal trading pre-clearance integrated.
