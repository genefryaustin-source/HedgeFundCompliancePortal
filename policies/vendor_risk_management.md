# Third-Party / Vendor Risk Management Policy (SOC 2 Security & Confidentiality)

**Purpose**: Manage risks associated with vendors and service providers that access or process company/client data.

**Requirements**:
- Pre-engagement risk assessment (security, privacy, financial stability).
- Review of vendor SOC 2, ISO 27001, or equivalent reports annually.
- Contracts must include data protection clauses, right-to-audit, and breach notification (72 hours).
- Ongoing monitoring of critical vendors (quarterly for high-risk).
- Termination procedures including data return/destruction.

**Risk Tiers**:
- High: Direct access to PII or production systems.
- Medium: Process non-sensitive data.
- Low: No data access.

**Responsibility**: CCO and Procurement.
