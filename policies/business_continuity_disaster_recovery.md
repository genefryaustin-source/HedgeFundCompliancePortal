# Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery (BCDR) Policy
**Objectives**: RTO < 4 hours for critical systems; RPO < 1 hour.
**Plan Elements**:
- Backup: Daily cloud backups (Google Drive + secondary provider).
- Failover: Redundant trading/NAV systems.
- Annual Testing: Table-top + live failover drill.
- Crisis Communication: Investor notification protocol.
**Link to Cybersecurity**: Integrated incident response.
