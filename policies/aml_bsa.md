## Pillar 1: Written Policies & Procedures (Risk-Based) – Expanded Risk Assessment
**Annual AML Risk Assessment** (conducted by BSA Officer, documented in portal, presented to senior management):

### Risk Assessment Methodology
1. **Inherent Risk Factors** (scored Low/Medium/High):
   - **Investor Base**: High-net-worth individuals, family offices, pension plans, non-U.S. residents, PEPs (politically exposed persons), funds-of-funds.
   - **Product/Service Risks**: Illiquid/alternative investments, derivatives, side pockets, subscription lines of credit.
   - **Geographic Risks**: Investors or counterparties in high-risk jurisdictions (FATF grey/black lists, OFAC-sanctioned countries).
   - **Transaction Risks**: Large/red-flag wires, rapid capital calls/distributions, inconsistent with stated strategy.

2. **Control Effectiveness** (mitigating factors scored):
   - CIP/CDD/EDD strength, OFAC screening frequency, ongoing monitoring tools, third-party administrator oversight.

3. **Residual Risk Rating**: Overall program risk = Inherent Risk minus Control Effectiveness.

### Detailed Risk Assessment Questionnaire (Portal-Integrated)
**Section A – Investor Risk (score each factor 1–5)**
- Does the investor reside in a high-risk jurisdiction? (Yes/No + details)
- Is the investor a PEP or close associate? (Screened via World-Check or equivalent)
- Beneficial ownership >25% unidentified or complex corporate structure?
- Source of wealth/funds documented and verified?

**Section B – Product & Transaction Risk**
- Fund strategy involves illiquid holdings or complex derivatives?
- Any side letters granting preferential liquidity/redemption?
- Unusual redemption patterns or early withdrawals?

**Section C – Operational & Third-Party Risk**
- Prime broker/administrator AML program reviewed annually?
- Cybersecurity controls sufficient to protect investor KYC data?

**Scoring & Action**:
- Total score ≥ 12 = High Risk → Mandatory EDD + enhanced monitoring.
- Document findings, mitigation plan, and senior-management approval in Google Drive.
- Re-assess within 30 days of material change (new investor type, regulatory update).

**Output**: Annual Risk Assessment Report generated via portal (PDF + DocuSign sign-off) and retained 5 years.

## Pillar 5: Customer Due Diligence, Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD), Recordkeeping & Reporting – Expanded

### Standard CDD (All Investors)
- Collect: Full legal name, address, TIN/EIN, date of birth/incorporation, source of funds/wealth, beneficial owners (≥25%).

### Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) – Triggered Automatically When Risk Score ≥12 (from Annual AML Risk Assessment)
**Triggers for EDD**:
- Investor is a PEP or close associate.
- Investor or beneficial owner in high-risk jurisdiction (FATF grey/black list, OFAC concerns).
- Complex corporate/trust structure with unidentified beneficial owners.
- Investment amount >$5M or represents >10% of fund AUM.
- Unusual transaction patterns (rapid inflows, inconsistent with stated strategy, side-letter requests for preferential terms).
- Negative media or sanctions hits.

**Mandatory EDD Procedures** (Documented in portal + Google Drive):
1. **In-Depth Source-of-Wealth Verification**: Bank statements, tax returns, audited financials, third-party wealth reports, or independent verification letters.
2. **Beneficial Ownership Deep Dive**: Corporate registry searches, ownership charts, and interviews with investor representatives.
3. **Adverse Media & Sanctions Rescreening**: Use World-Check, LexisNexis, or equivalent at onboarding + quarterly thereafter.
4. **Senior Management Approval**: BSA Officer + CEO sign-off via DocuSign before accepting subscription.
5. **Enhanced Ongoing Monitoring**: Monthly transaction reviews; automated alerts for red-flag activity; site visits or calls for high-risk investors.
6. **File Documentation**: All EDD materials uploaded to secure Google Drive folder with restricted access; retained 5 years minimum.
7. **Decline/Exit Strategy**: If EDD cannot be completed to satisfaction, subscription is declined or existing investment is redeemed with documented rationale.

**EDD Decision Log**: Maintained in portal (template form auto-generated on trigger).  
**Annual Review**: CCO tests sample EDD files during independent audit (Pillar 4).
