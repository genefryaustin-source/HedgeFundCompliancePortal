## 1. Scope
Applies to “Personal Information” of California residents (investors, employees, service providers) collected or processed by the firm.

## 2. Categories of Personal Information Collected
- Identifiers (name, address, email, TIN, IP address)
- Financial information (account numbers, investment amounts, source of funds)
- Internet/electronic activity (portal usage, email opens)
- Professional or employment-related information (for staff)
- Inferences drawn from the above

## 3. Purposes of Collection & Use
- AML/KYC compliance
- Subscription processing and investor reporting
- Marketing and client service
- Regulatory filings (Form ADV, Form PF)
- Internal operations and risk management

## 4. California Consumer Rights (CPRA)
- Right to Know / Access
- Right to Delete
- Right to Correct
- Right to Opt-Out of Sale / Sharing of Personal Information
- Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information
- Right to Non-Discrimination for Exercising Rights

**Response Timeline**: 45 days (extendable once by 45 days). All requests logged in portal with DocuSign audit trail.

## 5. Service Providers & Third Parties
- Contracts require CCPA-compliant Data Processing Agreements.
- No “sale” of personal information without explicit opt-out notice.

## 6. Data Security & Breach Notification
- Safeguards aligned with Reg S-P and Cybersecurity Policy.
- Breach notification to consumers within 45 days if risk of harm.

## 7. Training & Awareness
- Annual CCPA module in Training Center (quiz + DocuSign attestation).
